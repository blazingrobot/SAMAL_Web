About Us

About Our Firm

Samal Land Surveying is a trusted land surveying and mapping firm based in Davao City and Davao del Norte, committed to delivering accurate, timely, and regulation-compliant survey services. With years of experience in the industry, our team of licensed Geodetic Engineers leads the firm's technical operations, project execution, and client engagement.

Our Specializations

We specialize in relocation and boundary surveys, subdivision and consolidation, topographic and engineering surveys, survey returns for titling and registration, and the preparation of technical descriptions and legal documentation.

By integrating modern tools and technologies, we ensure precision and efficiency in every project. Samal Land Surveying works closely with landowners, developers, real estate professionals, and government offices to promote responsible land use and sustainable development across the region.
