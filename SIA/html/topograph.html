What is a Topographic Survey?

A topographic survey maps the contours, elevations, and features of a land area, providing essential data for architects, engineers, and developers. Our surveys include detailed information about terrain, vegetation, buildings, utilities, and other physical features.

Terrain Mapping

Accurate contour lines and elevation data

Structure Location

Precise positioning of existing structures

Utility Mapping

Location of underground and overhead utilities

Natural Features

Documentation of trees, water bodies, and vegetation

Common Applications:

  • Site planning and development
  • Architectural design
  • Engineering projects
  • Flood risk assessment
  • Landscape design
  • Construction planning
