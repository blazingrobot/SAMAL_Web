What is a Lot Verification Survey?

A Lot Verification Survey establishes and verifies the exact boundaries of a property, ensuring accurate property lines and preventing boundary disputes. Our certified surveyors provide precise measurements and documentation for legal and development purposes.

Boundary Marking

Precise identification and marking of property corners

Legal Documentation

Official survey plans for legal and registration purposes

Dispute Resolution

Settlement of boundary conflicts with accurate data

Title Support

Supporting documentation for land title applications

Common Applications:

  • Property boundary confirmation
  • Land title registration
  • Construction planning
  • Property subdivision
  • Boundary dispute resolution
  • Mortgage and loan requirements
