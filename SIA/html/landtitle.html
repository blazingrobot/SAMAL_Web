What are Land Title Applications?

Land Title Applications involve the preparation and processing of all necessary documents and survey plans required for land registration with the Registry of Deeds. Our experienced team guides you through the entire title application process.

Document Preparation

Complete preparation of legal and technical documents

Survey Plans

Accurate survey plans meeting registry requirements

Liaison Services

Coordination with government agencies and registries

Compliance Assurance

Ensuring all requirements are met for approval

Services Included:

  • Original registration applications
  • Transfer of title documentation
  • Subdivision plan preparation
  • Consolidation survey plans
  • Condominium plan registration
  • Annotation and correction proceedings
